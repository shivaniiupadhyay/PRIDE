Rules and Regulations for Impulse!

  • Oct 19-20,2016
    • •
  • Impulse
    • •
  • Dance


The one thing that certainly stood out as the celebration of the life is dance. It electrified the world and spread happiness in the best way of art possible. Now the focus is on you to make your move. Let the universe see the way you break it and set fire as you own the stage with your grace. Let it get the impulsive best out of you and leave the crowd ogle at your sway of a hand. Tune up the music in your body move to its undying rhythm.


The stage is all yours to set it on fire and paint an art that sets the IMPULSE in the mob.

Rules for Impulse:-
1. Registration fees: Rs 500/-per team
2. Time limit: Minimum- 4 minutes   Maximum- 11 minutes
3. Negative marking for exceeding the time limit.
4. Maximum 10 members can perform, Minimum 3 members are required to get your team Eventsed.
5. Choice of the song is open to the participants. The participants are requested to bring 2 CD’sto avoid technical disturbance and it should be in mp3 format.
6. Usage of props is allowed. The participants should carry their own props. No props will be provided by the event managers.
7. Obscenity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
8. The teams would be judged on the basis of their rhythm, formation, synchronization, expression, costumes, makeup and overall effects.
9. Decisions of the judges will be final and binding.
