Rules and Regulations for Rangmanch!

  • Oct 19-20,2016
    • •
  • Rangmanch
    • •
  • Drama

Theatre was created to showcase the truth about life and has been endlessly fascinating because it is so accidental; it is so much like life. If you have what it takes to entice the crowd and cast a spell with your story, the opportunity doesn’t get any better. We at Pride are giving the grandest “RANGMANCH” to tell your story your way.

A theme based dramatics competition inviting entries in either of the languages: Hindi or English.

Themes:
 Immortal Love stories….beginning from the end.
 Mythology in 21st century.

Rules for Rangmanch:

1. Registration fee: Rs.500/- per team.

2. Team can have minimum of 5 members and maximum of 10-12 members.

3. Time limit: 10-15 minutes.

4. No props or costumes will be provided by the organizers except for certain entities.

5. The teams would be judged on the basis of script, creativity, costumes, dialogue delivery,expression,stage covering,climax and overall effect.

6. Obscenity of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.

7. Decisions of the judges will be final and binding.
