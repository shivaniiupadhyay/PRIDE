Hospitality


Charges are as mentioned Below :

1. Accommodation: Rs 150/day

2. Caution money: Rs 100/- (Refundable)

3. Food charges: Rs 150/day (which covers breakfast, lunch and dinner).



NOTE*

In exceptional circumstances the refund amount may be forfeited subject to one or more of the following conditions:
i) Permanent damage has been done to the room/dorm/accessories where accommodation has been provided.
ii) Rooms are not vacated on or before 21st October 10:00 am.
The payment will be done at registration desk in cash/cheque at the Sir Padampat Singhania University campus, Udaipur. Refund has to be collected from accommodation desk. PRIDE is not liable to refund the amount in case of any mistake made by the person paying the amount.



Policy:

Everybody willing to avail accommodation in Pride must register on our website or can reach us on the campus. When you arrive at SPSU Udaipur, you have to come to accommodation desk, along with your college ID card. The list of all the students from a particular college must be present along with their ID proof. Your place of stay will be allotted along with your accommodation ID. Accommodation would be strictly on shared basis. Each student will be given accommodation only after it has been confirmed by the Hospitality Core Group Members. Students can avail accommodation facilities from 15th October 7:00 pm to 21st October 10:00 am.

Getting here:

The Sir Padampat Singhania University Campus is located about 10 Kms from the Maharana Pratap Udaipur Airport. You can hire a taxi from outside the airport to reach the campus in Bhatewar.
